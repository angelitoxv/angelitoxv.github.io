TSM Background

TSM is a multi-gaming organization which holds teams in many different eSports video games. The main game which holds a team is League of Legends. TSM Reginald, the founder of the organization, created the team as a means of competing at a professional level. However, as the team became a crowd favorite and excelled in the eSports scene the organization expanded to become an eSports titan. The team has grown and seen many different players come and go while maintaining a roster of strong players throughout every season. The League of Legends team has earned up to 4 regional championships and has attended the World Championship multiple times. Although the team has yet to be successful outside of the World's group stage, the organization continues the grow and dominate the regional scene.
